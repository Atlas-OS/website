# First setup

Welcome to AtlasOS! After your system restarts, you're ready to complete the final setup steps.

## Complete essential setup

Your system needs drivers and hardware optimization to work properly and perform at its best. Follow the Essential Setup guide for complete step-by-step instructions on installing drivers and optimizing your hardware.

## After essential setup

Once you've completed essential setup (drivers and hardware optimization), you can:

- **[Configure Atlas settings](/docs/atlas-configuration/)** - Customize Windows features and system preferences
- **[Explore optional tweaks](/docs/atlas-configuration/tweaks/)** - Personalize your interface and experience

<Callout type="tip">
  Take your time with essential setup. Getting your drivers and hardware configured properly will
  give you the best AtlasOS experience. Configuration and tweaks can be done afterward.
</Callout>

## Setup complete <IconInline name="tabler:check" size="md" />

After completing essential setup and configuring your preferences, you're all set! AtlasOS is configured and ready to use.

### What's next?

- **Explore [configuration options](/docs/atlas-configuration/)** to further customize your system
- **Learn about [upgrading AtlasOS](/docs/upgrade/)** when new versions are released
- **Check the [FAQ](/docs/faq/)** if you run into any issues or have questions
